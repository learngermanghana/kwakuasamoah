# Kwaku Travel & Relocation Website Structure

This plan is designed for a **personal-brand travel and relocation business** that converts social-media traffic into bookings and qualified inquiries.

## Recommended primary pages

1. **Home**
   - Clear value proposition (travel, relocation, and booking support)
   - Short founder intro and trust indicators
   - Quick CTAs: **Book Consultation**, **WhatsApp**, **Apply for Help**
   - Featured destinations/services and testimonial preview

2. **About Kwaku**
   - Story: migration from Ghana to the Netherlands through studies
   - Mission and audience served
   - Professional disclaimer (guidance/support, no false guarantees)

3. **Services**
   - Service cards with description, inclusions, and price/starting-from
   - Example services:
     - Study abroad guidance
     - Schengen visa preparation support
     - Relocation consultation (Netherlands/Europe)
     - Flight and hotel planning support
     - Document review and interview prep

4. **Packages**
   - Productized offers for clearer buying decisions
   - Example packages:
     - Netherlands Study Route Package
     - Schengen Visa Preparation Package
     - First-Time Traveler Starter Package
     - Custom Relocation Package

5. **Destinations**
   - Landing page plus destination-specific child pages
   - Suggested destinations: Netherlands, Germany, Belgium, France, Spain, UK
   - Each page should include visa basics, cost expectations, common mistakes, and related services

6. **Book Consultation**
   - Conversion-focused booking form
   - Fields: name, email/phone, country, preferred destination, travel dates, budget, travelers, notes
   - Optional paid booking fee and calendar scheduling

7. **Blog / Travel Updates**
   - SEO-focused content hub for relocation and travel updates
   - Suggested topics:
     - Schengen document checklists
     - Netherlands study-route guidance
     - Visa mistakes to avoid
     - Cost breakdowns for moving to Europe

8. **Testimonials / Success Stories**
   - Video testimonials, screenshots, and case stories
   - Highlight outcomes and service used

9. **FAQ**
   - Answer frequent trust and process questions
   - Include policy-facing questions: approvals, timelines, refunds, communication channels

10. **Contact**
    - Contact form + WhatsApp CTA
    - Business email, hours, and links to social channels

11. **Policies**
    - Privacy Policy
    - Terms of Service
    - Refund/Cancellation Policy
    - Service Disclaimer

12. **Media / Social Hub**
    - Embedded YouTube content and links to TikTok/Instagram/Facebook
    - “Follow Kwaku” and “Start your process” CTAs

## Suggested top navigation

- Home
- About
- Services
- Packages
- Destinations
- Book Consultation
- Blog
- Testimonials
- FAQ
- Contact

## Priority conversion flow

1. User discovers Kwaku on social media.
2. User lands on Home or a Blog/Destination page.
3. User clicks **Book Consultation** or **WhatsApp**.
4. Lead captured in inquiry system.
5. Follow-up funnels into service/package purchase.

## Positioning statement

> **Relocate, Travel, and Explore Europe with Confidence**  
> Guidance, bookings, and trusted travel updates from Kwaku.
